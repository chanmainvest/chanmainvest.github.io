Live · Open-source

Portfolio Dashboard

A Bloomberg-style single-page report that reads an Excel workbook, pulls live market data from Yahoo Finance, and produces an interactive analytics dashboard for your real portfolio.

Risk that you can actually read

VaR, Sharpe, Sortino, Calmar, max drawdown and beta — in both CAD and USD, with hover-explainers on every term.

Correlation + stress testing

Heatmaps show how the book really moves together, while stress scenarios from -50% to +50% reveal how the portfolio behaves under pressure.

Options-aware exposure + privacy mode

Live option chains, delta exposure, privacy mode for screen-sharing, and dual-currency reporting for every dollar figure.

Investment Tutorial

A 52-week investment course taking you from "why invest?" to professional-grade portfolio management, grounded in the CFA curriculum and decades of real retail trading experience.

Interactive learning from week 1

The first week already includes adjustable charts and guided comparisons, so the course teaches by reading, exploring and testing ideas rather than by static notes alone.

Five levels, 52 weeks

Foundation, Intermediate, Advanced, Sophisticated, Expert — each lesson combines structured reading, interactive charts, and a two-host YouTube-ready script.

Still a first draft, already multilingual

This is the first draft, not the finished course. Even so, it already ships in four language versions and is designed to grow into a fuller interactive learning system.
